Which Australian dog refuges do you support?

We rotate our partnerships quarterly to spread the love, working with certified no-kill shelters across NSW, QLD, VIC, and beyond. Each quarter, we spotlight a different refuge on our social media, sharing their stories and the pups you've helped save.

How can I track where my donation goes?

Transparency is key! We publish quarterly "Paws & Waves Reports" on our website and Instagram, complete with photos, donation receipts, and heartwarming adoption success stories. You'll see exactly how your bikini purchase transformed a dog's life.

Can I nominate a dog refuge for partnership?

Absolutely! We love hearing from our community. Send us your nomination through our contact form with details about the refuge. If they align with our values and meet our criteria, they could be our next quarterly partner.

Do you organize beach meetups with rescue dogs?

Yes! We host "Bikinis & Puppies" beach days in major coastal cities where Solumi swimmers can meet adoptable dogs from our partner refuges. It's the perfect blend of sun, surf, and potential soulmates (the four-legged kind).
